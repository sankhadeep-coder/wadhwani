Venture Viability Analysis

The 1,100 TWh Problem

The 2024 forecasted demand for electricity is growing by 4.3% (~1,100 TWh). Buildings currently consume ~30% of world energy. Rising demand causes pollution and reliance on non-renewable sources.

The Smart Solution

Our piezoelectric and electromagnetic tiles capture energy from crowds in public spaces. Every footstep generates up to 37.5 Watts, which is monitored via real-time microcontrollers.

Target Infrastructure

Designed primarily for Urban Government Authorities. Perfect for high-footfall zones like metro stations, sidewalks, and malls, turning wasted kinetic movement into a clean resource.
